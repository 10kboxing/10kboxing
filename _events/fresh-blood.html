10K Boxing presents Fresh Blood an action packed night filled with white collar & Semi pro boxing showing some of Brighton's hottest prospects such as Freedom Eubank's and Matar Sambou. This is a family friendly event so bring you're loved ones as there are junior's fighting on the show.

Doors open at 5 5.30pm

First fight 6pm 

 West African Food will be available on the night.

Ticket Options

Standing Tickets are available at £20 for over 16's or Ringside £35 or VIP Tables £400 

Our VIP Table option seats up to 10 people, includes Waitress service and Ringside seats.
