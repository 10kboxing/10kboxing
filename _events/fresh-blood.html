Ticket Options

Standing Tickets are available at £20 or Ringside £35 or VIP Tables £400. 

Our VIP Table option seats up to 10 people, includes Waitress service and Ringside seats.

Dresscode

We operate a VERY strict dresscode policy - Please Dress to Impress.

Are there ID requirements or an age limit to enter the event?

Yes you must be 18 or over to enter one of our events ID May be requested on the door.

 

Is my registration/ticket transferrable?

No, Tickets are not transferable

Do I have to bring my printed ticket to the event?

There will be a list at the door which will include the names of people who have purchased online, please bring your mobile so that we can check your online ticket. 

 

What is the refund policy?

We do not offer any refunds and tickets are non transfereable.
