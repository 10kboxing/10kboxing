Promoters FAQ:

  • How do I book a Boxer?

Simply give us a call on 07533780033 or email us to recieve a call within 24 hours to discuss your booking.

 

  • When do I pay for the booking

Once you are happy with the matchmaking,we require a 20% upfront fee to secure your bookings. The rest of the payment will be requested upon arrival of the boxing event.

 

  • I have more than 1 Event, can you match more?

We aim to match as many fights as possible and by staying in constant communication our target is to recruit and match the best possible fights for the promoter to sure a great action packed night. We can provide boxers for your future events just simply let us know the date and requirements.

 

Boxer’s FAQ:

  • Why join 10K Boxing agency.

10K Boxing is the home of white collar & semi pro boxing, working for the fighter and promoter to provide the best possible match ups for a great fair 50/50 fight. Weather you have just started training or have had 50 fights, we can help match the best fights at the right time of your career.

  • How do I sign up to the boxing agency?

You can simply fill out the signup form (HERE) to receive a call within 48 hours to discuss your aims and targets to help push you in the right direction or simply call Bady on 07533780033 NOW.

  • Will I get matched fairly.

We put safety first every time we let a boxer step in the ring so we do not tolerate any boxer lying about their weights or expeierneces becuase we match boxers according to their weight and experiences so all boxer are expected to tell the truth about their training and experience & their record to ensure their safety as well as the opponent.

  • How much do I get paid for fighting/Who pays me?

Each purse would be different depending on the location,experience and your opponent. You will be told the purse when offer gets made so you can tell us if you want to take the fight or not. You get paid straight after the fight.

  • Do I have to sell tickets to fight?

It would help to cover cost of running the show if you could sell any tickets but how ever if you can not sell any tickets you are guaranteed to still fight and get paid the agreed amount for that bout.

  • Lying about record and experience.

We believe safety is first in any job so that why we go that extra mile to make sure everyone is safe while they are in the ring. You wouldn't want to step in the ring and get badly beaten up because someone lied about their weight just to make a quick buck. We can not stress that we do not tolerate boxers lying about their weight. If we find out that any boxer has lied about their weight they will be banned from fighting on any of our shows and we will warn all the promoters we work with to ensure they don't work with the boxer who puts others life at rise for money.

  • Drugs Policy.

We do not tolerate drugs..
